Fix ragged rows

When you open a data file, it may contain an inconsistent number of columns across each row. This is called "ragged rows" and does not represent valid tabluar data. When you validate a table, ragged rows will be detected. Comma Chameleon can fix ragged rows.

Once ragged rows are corrected, you'll be able to validate and share your data.
